Titan — Analysis

On-demand analysis. Invoked only via Sentinel. Advisory outputs only; no commands, no outcome override.

Role

Titan is invoked by Sentinel with a reason and trace id. It does not issue commands or override Sentinel decisions.

  • Assumptions and confidence
  • Weak points and alternative explanations
  • Risk weighting
  • Recommended follow-up questions

Output types

Assumptions What Titan assumes.
Weak points Gaps or uncertainties.
Alternatives Other explanations.
Risk Weighting and severity.
Questions Recommended questions.
